Our Services

AENS provides integrated engineering, procurement, construction, and maintenance services for public and private clients across Africa.

Core Areas of Expertise

Civil & Structural Engineering

Design and construction of civil structures, foundations, bridges, and roads with an emphasis on durability and cost efficiency.

Electrical Engineering

Turnkey power solutions including system design, installation of substations, cabling, automation, and renewable energy systems.

Mechanical & Fabrication

Fabrication, installation, and maintenance of piping, pressure vessels, tanks, and mechanical systems for industrial clients.

Specialized Capabilities

Instrumentation & Automation

Control systems design, installation, calibration, and integration for industrial processes.

Procurement & Supply

Sourcing, inspection, and delivery of technical equipment and materials for construction and plant projects.

Project Management

Scheduling, cost control, risk management, and coordination across multi-disciplinary teams.

HSE Compliance

Strict adherence to safety and environmental standards to ensure zero-incident operations.

Maintenance & Reliability

Preventive and corrective maintenance for industrial facilities and energy systems.

Training & Technical Support

Upskilling programs for client teams on engineering tools, standards, and operational safety.
